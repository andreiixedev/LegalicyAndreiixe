=====To install the graphics=====
Extract the parts with winrar and open the folder ATI Drivers 
--> Click to the Folder "Double driver" 
--> Click dd.exe (run as administrator) 
--> Click Restore 
--> Locate Backup 
--> Olther location 
--> Locate the Driver "ATI Drivers" 
( Click "Ati Radeon Xpress 200m Drivers" => "IBM ThinkPad R51e" => "Display" => Select "ATI Radeon Xpress Series" => Click OK) 
--> Click OK 
--> Click restore Now

!If something appears in red, press yes install driver
